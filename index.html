About Us

MindBits is a quartely published magazine by Technocrat Coders. We write our opinions and views on various fields of interest like

  • Technology for society
  • Latest news regarding science & tech
  • Awareness about coding & programming

Technocrat Coders is an initiative taken by Khwab Foundation and supported by LND College, Motihari to promote coding culture in the rural and backward regions of India. We share our learnings to encourage our upcoming generations about software & IT industry

Our main vision is to develop a coding environment where people think, discuss and solve real life problems to uplift our society
